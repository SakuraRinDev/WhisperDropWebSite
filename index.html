Features

Simple. Unshakable.

All you need is a single shortcut. Speak, and the words arrive exactly where your cursor waits.

Ctrl + Shift + Space
I.

Entirely Local

Speech is processed on your device. No internet, no cloud uploads. Your words remain yours.

II.

One Shortcut

Hold Ctrl + Shift + Space, speak, and release. The text is inserted at your cursor.

III.

LLM Refinement

Choose Claude, OpenAI, or a local Ollama model to polish your dictation into natural prose.

IV.

Your Vocabulary

Register jargon, names, and product terms. The more you use it, the better it listens.

V.

Waveform Presence

A beautiful waveform appears while recording — a quiet visual confirmation that your voice is heard.

VI.

Memory & Recall

Every transcription is saved, searchable, and ready to be reused. Past voices become your archive.
